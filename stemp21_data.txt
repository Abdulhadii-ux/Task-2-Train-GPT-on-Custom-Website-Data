About – stemp`
At STEPM21, our mission is to foster a transformative learning environment that encourages individuals to discover and develop their hidden skills in science, technology, engineering, mathematics, and programming. Through innovative educational programs and resources, we aim to equip our students with the tools, knowledge, and confidence needed to excel in the rapidly evolving world. By awakening their potential, we inspire creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities, enabling them to make significant contributions to society and shape a better tomorrow
STEMP21 Mission
STEMP21 is a leading education company dedicated to providing comprehensive learning opportunities in the fields of science, technology, engineering, mathematics, and programming (STEMP) for the 21st century. Our primary focus is to awaken the hidden potential in students and equip them with the skills necessary to thrive in an increasingly digital and technology-driven world
STEMP Scratch Books
One of our flagship offerings is the STEMP Scratch book, a unique educational resource tailored for students from Grade 1 to Grade 8. These meticulously designed books align with various curriculum frameworks, including the common core curriculum, Cambridge curriculum, and single national curriculum, ensuring that students receive a well-rounded education in line with their academic requirements.
The STEMP Scratch book series provides an engaging and interactive learning experience, integrating real-world connections through programming skills and 21st-century skills across core subjects such as science, mathematics, and computers. The books are carefully structured to introduce students to fundamental concepts while nurturing their critical thinking, problem-solving, and computational thinking abilities.
Each book within the STEMP Scratch series offers a dynamic blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, enabling students to understand complex concepts and apply them to real-life scenarios. Through hands-on exercises, coding challenges, and project-based learning, students gain a deep understanding of how STEM disciplines intersect and their relevance to the world around them
.
Real Life Connection
STEMP21 is committed to ensuring that students not only acquire technical proficiency but also develop essential 21st-century skills, including collaboration, creativity, communication, and adaptability. By equipping students with these skills alongside their core subject knowledge, we empower them to become well-rounded individuals capable of navigating the challenges and opportunities of the modern world.

stemp-lab – stemp`
STEMP Lab, we believe in empowering students with programming skills, nurturing 21st-century competencies, and instilling a growth mindset. Through the integration of STEMP Scratch Books and our Computer Lab, we aim to create an engaging and impactful learning environment that equips students with the skills they need for success in an ever-evolving world. In STEMP Lab where we introduce you to two exciting components: STEMP Scratch Books and our Computer Lab.
S
T
EMP Scratch Books:
STEMP Scratch Books are programming-based books designed to foster STEM concepts. With a comprehensive range from Grade 1 to 8, these books align with three prominent curricula: Common Core (USA), Cambridge (UK), and Single National Curriculum. Each book comprises 20 Scratch programming-based STEMP guided projects and 20 STEMP challenges. Notably, every project is aligned with the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals (UNO 2030 SDGs), encouraging students to connect their learning with real-world issues.
These books serve as comprehensive resources that integrate programming skills, 21st-century skills, and a growth mindset among students. They complement subjects such as Science, Math, Computers, and Social Sciences, enhancing cross-disciplinary learning and promoting a holistic educational experience.
STEMP Python Books:
Introducing STEM Python Books, an innovative series tailored for students from Grade 9 to 12, designed to foster comprehensive learning in the fields of biology, mathematics, physics, and chemistry. Each grade-level book comprises 20 meticulously crafted guided projects per subject, offering a dynamic approach to understanding key concepts.
Within these pages, students will embark on a journey that goes beyond traditional education, delving into the realms of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and the Internet of Things. Through hands-on exploration and practical application, learners will not only grasp fundamental principles but also develop critical problem-solving skills essential for navigating the complexities of the modern world.
STEM Python Books doesn’t just provide knowledge; it cultivates a mindset of innovation and creativity, empowering students to become adept in cutting-edge technologies and prepared for the challenges of tomorrow. With 20 stimulating STEMP challenges integrated into each book, students are encouraged to think outside the box, collaborate, and push the boundaries of their understanding.
Prepare your students for success in the digital age with STEM Python Books, where learning transcends the classroom and opens doors to limitless possibilities
Computer Lab:
To support the implementation of the STEMP Scratch Books, STEMP21 provides a dedicated Computer Lab. Recognizing the need for practical application and hands-on learning, our computer lab ensures that students can actively engage with the offline Scratch software. With a ratio of 1 laptop for every 30 students, we create an environment conducive to experiential learning and collaborative programming.
The computer lab serves as a space for students to explore, experiment, and apply their programming knowledge gained from the STEMP Scratch Books. It facilitates interactive learning, fosters creativity, and empowers students to develop essential digital literacy skills
Teacher Training:
STEMP21 offers systematic teacher training, ensuring comprehensive understanding. Each teacher receives a unique ID, enabling flexible learning environments. Covering project books, software usage, SLOs, and project alignment with SDGs. Upon completion, teachers attain STEM certification. Empowering educators for effective STEMP21 implementation
